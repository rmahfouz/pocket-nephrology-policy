Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 3, 2026

Pocket Nephrology ("we," "our," or "the App") is dedicated to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

  • Personal Data: Pocket Nephrology does not require a user account, and we do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.

2. Children’s Privacy

Our App is designed for medical professionals and students. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. This App does not target children as its primary audience.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services (such as Google Play Services or Firebase) that may collect information used to identify you for the purpose of app stability and analytics. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

4. Security

We value your trust. Since we do not collect personal data, the risk of data breaches involving your personal information is minimized. However, we encourage users to keep their device software updated for maximum security.
